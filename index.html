ABOUT US


"The most memorable rest time starts here. "

Nestled amidst the dense greenery of Nagarkot, Hotel Mayur just like its name has the tranquil surrounding and peaceful ambient. The hotel situated on top of quaint villages and terraced ﬁelds, rising above the misty clouds that surround the hills round.



Overlooking the valley beyond, Hotel Mayur has the perfect mixture of modern luxury and healing nature. Guests will be able to enjoy best of amenities and services on the nature’s lap. The Hotel gives the perfect blend of luxurious service to be pampered with also to get closer to the nature through nature walks, hiking and other adventurous activities.

One of the most appreciated hotel


Our upscale transformation has consistently led our hotel to be rated among the best, based on user reviews.

OUR ROOMS

Every room faces the breathtaking view of the Himalayas and has a balcony terrace to enjoy the view and soak up some sun. Breathe in the fresh air and enjoy the greenery. Some of the rooms even have a private garden to relax in your own small piece of heaven.
